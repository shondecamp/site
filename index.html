About Me

I’ve been working with cross-functional teams on digital enterprise solutions for 7+ years. I have designed mobile, responsive web, and desktop applications in the government, telecommunications, and healthcare spaces. That design work has included full-cycle UX responsibilities including research, wireframes, user-testing, mock-ups, iterative design, prototypes and much more. I’ve developed a strong knowledge of human-computer interaction (HCI) and user-centered design. Ultimately, my design work delights users, meets business objectives, and operates within the technical and other constraints of my projects.

My family and I enjoy the work and fun of our hobby farm. We like to ride horses, raise bunnies, chase chickens, fix fences, and all the other projects that come with owning a couple of extra acres. We frequently find ourselves in the mountains hiking or camping. We also manage to make several summer trips to cool off in the lake.
