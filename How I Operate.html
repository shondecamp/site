How I Operate as a Staff Designer

I work at the intersection of product strategy, UX design, and delivery. My role typically goes beyond interface design to shaping product direction and ensuring teams ship usable, effective solutions.

Problem Framing
I help teams clarify ambiguous or high-risk problem spaces by:

  • Identifying user and business outcomes
  • Mapping workflows and system constraints
  • Defining success criteria
  • Establishing a clear design approach

Prototyping & Exploration
I work closely with product managers, engineers, and leadership to:

  • Low- and high-fidelity Figma prototypes
  • Workflow and system modeling
  • AI-assisted prototype generation
  • Rapid concept validation with stakeholders

Cross-Functional Influence
I use prototyping as a thinking and alignment tool:

  • Align on priorities and scope
  • Influence roadmap and product direction
  • Navigate constraints and tradeoffs
  • Maintain focus on user and business outcomes

Delivery & Quality
I stay engaged through implementation to ensure:

  • Design intent is preserved
  • Edge cases are resolved
  • Systems remain coherent
  • Shipped experiences meet quality standards
